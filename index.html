Hi there, My name is Samuel Woiye. Your run of the mill self-taught software developer. Born and and raised in Nakuru. Currently leaving in Kambi Somali centre near Bahati. I have a passion for technology that cannot be quenched and in those rare moments I feel like giving up I remember I quote from arthur buddhold, "follow your passion and success will follow you". Thus thats my story, not the end though but just a minuscule beginning to an ever growing story. The future is yet to come!
